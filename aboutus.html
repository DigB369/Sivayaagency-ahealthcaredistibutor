ABOUT US!

Sivaya Agency is a healthcare distribution agency that supplies different types of body Implants and Disposables to hospitals all over Odisha, India. In addition to that we also provide, various general surgical instruments which are used during OT to the hospitals. Moreover we also offer technical support through our marketing and surgery assist staff with regards to the usage of our products, during the process of OT.

Our main objective is to get connected with as many surgeons as possible, and provide them with the necessary requirements within a stipulated period of time.

Our Office is Mainly based in Bhubaneswar, the capital of Odisha, So as to address every part of the state without any issues.
